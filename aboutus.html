About us

We are the "flyest" birds in the nest offering you the flexibility to host and monetize your live and virtual events anywhere in the world. We are passionate about helping creators make real money. At Raven, we want to democratize entertainment and make premium live video content like music concerts, fashion events, games, religious events, comedy shows and all types of content available on the go to billions of people all over the world.
