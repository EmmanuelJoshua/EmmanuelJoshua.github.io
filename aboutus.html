About us

We are the "flyest" birds in the nest offering you the flexibility to host and monetize your live and virtual events anywhere in the world. We are passionate about helping creators make real money. At Raven, we want to democratize entertainment and make premium live video content like music concerts, fashion events, games, religious events, comedy shows and all type of content available on the go to billions of people all over the world.

Here are some common questions we get asked often:
I’m new, how does Raven work?

Hey, welcome to Raven Livestream. Thanks downloading our amazing app, you simply have to create an event put in all the details of the event and set ticket prices and live pay slots. That’s it. Share your event with your friends and fans to have them join the event. Sell tickets and make money. If you are not a creator, you simply can search for events, buy tickets and have a wonderful time on the app.

How can i make money on Raven?
You can make money by selling tickets to your events or setting up live-pay slots which can be used for donations, voting, selling digital and physical products. Head on to create an event and explore these options. Also, partnering with sponsors can put a little more money in your wallet.
How do I let people know I have an event ?
Cool, so you’ve created an event. To let people know you have an event, simple go to your event description, copy the event link and simply share it on any other platform. It’s that simple!
Can i cancel an event?
Yes you can! Scroll to your manage events page, click on the event and cancel the event. Don’t worry about people who have gotten tickets. They will automatically receive a refund once you cancel your event.
How do i fund my wallet?
This is probably the smoothest process. Head over to your wallet, hit the deposit button. Fill in your country and amount and follow the process and that’s it. You should get two emails confirming your deposit
Can i withdraw my money to my bank account?
This is even cooler! Your entire withdrawal process is up to you. You can get paid to your PayPal account or directly into your bank account. Get into your wallet, press the withdraw button, fill in your details and in a few business days, we will process your withdrawal and notify you immediately.
Can i be a sponsor?
Yes you can! Anyone can be a sponsor, creator and user. Simply search for any events that you like, check the description and click the “become a sponsor” button. You will immediately start chatting with the event creator and you can make super sweet deals instantly from anywhere in the world.
How safe is Raven!
We try our best to give you the most secure and safest app you can possibly find on the market. We don’t store and share any single bit of your information to third party services and we don’t do any form of advertising on the app for now. Our payment services are safe and secure.
Can i share tickets?
You’re already awesome. To share tickets, you first have to purchase the ticket, then go to your tickets page, click on the ticket and click share. Share your tickets with your friends or anyone directly on the Raven Livestream app. simply search for their handle and that it.

To change your username and name, just head over to your profile and edit it. It’s that simple. Kindly remember that only one user can have a unique username (@handle)
